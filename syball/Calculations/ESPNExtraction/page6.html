2017 Outlook: Due to being the third overall pick in 2013, the bar was set very high for Porter and by those lofty expectations, his career averages of 7.5 points, 3.6 rebounds, and 1.0 assists are very underwhelming. Regardless of the sport, fantasy is all about opportunity and the Wizards seem willing to give him that (30.3 minutes per game last season and no real threat to cut into that this season). Thanks to advanced metrics, the NBA has turned into a league where a "good" shot is either a layup or a three pointer, making it encouraging that Porter's percentage of "good" shots increased last season as he saw his spike in playing time and his conversion rates did as well. It may not seem overwhelming, but there were only seven players last season that averaged 1.5 3PM, six rebounds, and two assists last season, a stat line that is well within reach for Porter in his fourth season.
